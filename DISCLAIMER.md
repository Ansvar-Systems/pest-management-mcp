# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides pest management data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional pest management, agronomic, or pesticide application advice. Treatment recommendations, product approvals, and IPM thresholds are based on published UK government and AHDB sources, but individual field conditions, resistance profiles, and local regulations vary. Always consult a qualified agronomist or BASIS-qualified advisor before making crop protection decisions.

## Pesticide Data

Pesticide approval data is sourced from the HSE Chemicals Regulation Division (CRD) register. **Always check the current CRD register (https://www.hse.gov.uk/pesticides/databases/) before purchasing or applying any pesticide product.** Product approvals can be revoked or amended at any time. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **AHDB Knowledge Library** -- Agriculture and Horticulture Development Board

## Currency

Pest management data changes. Product approvals are amended monthly. Resistance profiles shift over seasons. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
